Weather Site Plan

Home Revised Plan

Purpose Statement

The main reason that I am building this website is so that people can have a reliable source to go to to find information about the weather. Its purpose is to provide people with up to date weather information so that they can plan accordingly. The other content on the website will serve the purpose of educating users about various weather-related news and topics.

Audience Description

  • Age: 18-95 years old
  • Income: Varies between users
  • Education: Varies between users
  • Lifestyle: They spend a good amount of time outdoors
  • Geographic Locations:
    • Franklin, ID
    • Greenville, SC
    • Springfield, IL
  • They will be accessing the site through mobile phones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers. They generally will be using a hi-speed wireless Internet connection, but the occasional user will be accessing the site through his/her cellphone network.
